Marco Amato

Ph.D. Student

Marco was born in Bologna on January 11th, 1993. His educational background includes technical high school at Istituto Tecnico per Geometri "Pier-Crescenzi Pacinotti," where he was introduced to structural mechanics for the first time. He then pursued both a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Civil Engineering at the University of Bologna. Currently, he is a Ph.D. student at the University of Trento.

While working on his Bachelor's thesis, he conducted an experimental campaign at the University of Bologna laboratory. For his Master's thesis, he pursued a research internship abroad at Florida Atlantic University, focusing on the weighted residual method for stepped structures.
